Progress

Our progress so far has been centered on research. Our project involves creating a graph of an indoor space. We have been looking into the best ways to represent multiple floors in our graph and what existing technologies could be useful in creating our project. Below are the sources we have explored.

  • Study on directed indoor path-finding
  • Google Distance Matrix API
  • iNav Indoor Navigation Model
  • Indoor Spatial Analysis Using Space Syntax
  • Standards of Indoor Spatial Information

Our next step will be mapping out our possible paths inside the SL Building.
